Our story

Your Local Pest Control Partner Since 2008

To provide Dallas families and businesses with safe, effective, and affordable pest control solutions that protect their property and peace of mind.

Parsons Pest Management Store was founded in 2008 by owner Mike Parsons, a Dallas native who saw a need for honest, reliable pest control in the community. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to quality, Mike built a company known for its thoroughness and transparency. Over the years, we've grown to a team of 12 certified technicians, but we've never lost our small-business dedication to customer satisfaction.

Today, we serve thousands of homes and businesses across the Dallas metro area. Our approach combines the latest pest control technology with old-fashioned customer service. We believe in educating our clients, using eco-friendly products when possible, and standing behind every job with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. When you call Parsons, you're not just a customer — you're a neighbor.
