Our story

Your Local Dallas Pest Control Experts

To provide safe, effective, and affordable pest control that protects the health and property of our Dallas community.

Parsons Pest Management Store was founded in 1998 by John Parsons, a Dallas native who saw a need for reliable, honest pest control in his neighborhood. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to customer service, John built a business based on trust and results. Over the years, we've grown into a team of certified technicians serving thousands of homes and businesses across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

Today, we remain family-owned and locally operated. Every technician undergoes rigorous training and uses the latest industry techniques. We believe in treating every customer like a neighbor — with respect, transparency, and a dedication to getting the job done right. Our goal is simple: keep your home pest-free and give you peace of mind.
