Overview

Termites cause billions in damage each year. Our termite control program includes thorough inspections, liquid soil treatments, and baiting systems to eliminate colonies and prevent future infestations. We use Sentricon® and Termidor® for proven results.

What's included

  • Annual termite inspection included
  • Advanced baiting or liquid barrier options
  • Complete colony elimination guarantee
  • Transferable warranty for peace of mind

How it works

1

Inspection

We perform a detailed inspection using moisture meters and infrared tools to detect termite activity.

2

Treatment Plan

Based on the infestation type, we design a treatment plan using bait stations or liquid termiticide.

3

Monitoring

We monitor bait stations quarterly and retreat as needed until the colony is eliminated.

Common questions

How do I know if I have termites?

Signs include mud tubes, hollow-sounding wood, and discarded wings. We offer free inspections to confirm.

How long does termite treatment take?

Initial treatment can be completed in one day. Baiting systems may take a few weeks to fully eliminate the colony.
