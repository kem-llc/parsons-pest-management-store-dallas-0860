Bed Bug Treatment

Fast, effective bed bug elimination using heat and chemical treatments.

Schedule this service

Overview

Bed bugs are notoriously difficult to eliminate. Our bed bug treatment combines heat therapy, steam, and targeted chemical applications to kill bed bugs at all life stages. We also provide preparation guidance and follow-up inspections to ensure complete eradication.

What's included

  • Same-day emergency service available
  • Heat treatment reaches all hiding spots
  • Chemical treatment for residual protection
  • Follow-up inspection to confirm elimination

How it works

1

Inspection

We use canine detection and visual inspection to locate all harborages.

2

Treatment

We apply heat treatment (120°F+) and steam to kill bugs and eggs, followed by a residual chemical spray.

3

Follow-Up

We return in 14 days for a re-inspection and second treatment if necessary.

Common questions

Do I need to leave my home during treatment?

Yes, for heat treatment you must vacate for 6-8 hours. Chemical treatments require a few hours.

How many treatments are needed?

Most infestations require 1-2 treatments. We guarantee elimination with our two-visit protocol.
